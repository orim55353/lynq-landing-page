Features

Everything you need to hire well

One place for your jobs, candidates, and hiring data. No spreadsheets required.

Job Posting

Create listings in minutes. Track impressions and apply rates for every role you publish.

Candidate Pipeline

Move candidates through stages — from applied to hired — without losing anyone along the way.

Hiring Analytics

See your funnel, traffic sources, and trends at a glance. Know what's working and what's not.

Sponsored Campaigns

Boost your job listings to reach more candidates. Set a budget, launch, and track ROI in real time.

Market Insights

Benchmark salaries and understand talent supply in your market so you can make competitive offers.

Billing & Credits

Transparent pay-as-you-go pricing with credit management, invoices, and spend tracking built in.

Product

See what you'll be working with

A hiring dashboard that's actually pleasant to use — from pipeline management to performance tracking.

Pipeline

Visual candidate flow

Drag-and-drop candidates through customizable stages. Never lose track of where anyone stands.
