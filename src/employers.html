Features

Everything you need to hire well

One place for your jobs, candidates, and hiring data. No spreadsheets required.

Job Posting

Create listings in minutes. Track impressions and apply rates for every role you publish.

Candidate Pipeline

Move candidates through stages — from applied to hired — without losing anyone along the way.

Hiring Analytics

See your funnel, traffic sources, and trends at a glance. Know what's working and what's not.

Sponsored Campaigns

Boost your job listings to reach more candidates. Set a budget, launch, and track ROI in real time.

Market Insights
