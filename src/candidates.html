How It Works

From download to applied in under 3 minutes

No resumes. No cover letters. No 45-minute application forms.

1

Tell us what you want

Pick your role, location, salary range, and how you like to work. Takes about 90 seconds.

2

Browse your matches

We show you jobs that match your profile. Swipe right on the ones you like, left on the ones you don't. The more you swipe, the better your feed gets.

3

Tap to apply

See a role you like? One tap sends your profile to the company. Done. Move on to the next one.

Why Lynq

Job hunting, minus everything you hate about it

Every job shows how well it fits you. Stop guessing if you're qualified, the score tells you.
